Pillar 01

Faster delivery of bugs & routine tasks

Bugs sit 5 days before a human opens them — measured across 206 issues in 2 KA teams. 80% of all ≤1SP bugs are automatable — engineers lose focus re-triaging tickets a machine can own.

Ka-Minion picks up any ≤1SP ticket the moment it lands in Jira. Reads context, writes fix, runs CI, opens PR — 24/7, no queue, no handoff.

5d → <3hPhase 1 gate KPI · bug resolution time · 40× faster · n=206 issues
Pillar 02

Zombie task closure — healthy backlog, zero noise

1 in 5 Jira tickets is a zombie (>30 days). Across 43 teams this is hundreds of stale issues inflating estimates and burying real work every sprint.

Sentinel mode runs nightly: scans stale tickets, auto-enriches context, resolves if automatable, flags for human decision if not. Backlog stays clean by default — no engineer needed.

22% → <8%Phase 2 KPI · stale ticket rate across all active teams
Pillar 03

Out-of-hours duty calls & alerts — engineers get their weekends back

Engineers rotate on-duty shifts covering nights, weekends & bank holidays. ~3 hands-on calls & alerts per team per week — restarts, rollbacks, known error patterns — pulling engineers away from personal time at real cost (on-call premium + burnout risk).

Ka-Minion Sentinels watch 24/7 — monitoring, triaging & silencing false alerts automatically. When a known pattern fires they act immediately: restart, rollback, silence alert, update status page. Only genuine unknowns wake a human. Engineers stay off-duty unless it truly matters.

−30%Phase 2 KPI · hands-on out-of-hours calls & alerts per team per week
Scope guardrail: Ka-Minions only touch ≤1SP issues. No merges to main without passing CI + Paperclip audit gate. Human approval for anything above scope. PoC running inside Banana Squad.
Cost of waiting: Each quarter without Ka-Minions = ~€557K in recoverable capacity lost across all 43 teams. The zombie rate grows ~2pp/quarter at current trend.

Governance & risk mitigation
Scope guardrail: Ka-Minions only act on ≤1 Story Point issues. No merge to main without passing CI + Paperclip audit gate. Hard-coded upper limit enforced at orchestration layer.
Full audit trail: every agent action (code read, diff generated, PR opened) is logged in Paperclip with timestamp, token cost, and outcome. Exportable for compliance review.
Kill switch: any Ka-Minion can be paused or terminated instantly from the Paperclip dashboard — per-agent, per-team, or org-wide. No runaway agents.
LLM hallucination risk: mitigated by requiring CI green + reviewer approval before any merge. Ka-Minion proposes; human (or automated gate) approves.
API cost overrun risk: per-agent monthly budget caps in Paperclip. Teams cannot exceed allocated token spend without explicit override from their EM.
Phase-gated investment: no further spend unlocks without hitting the prior phase's KPI. Zero risk of over-investing before value is proven.
